# How to make Pancakes

## Servings: 7

## Time to prepare: 
15 mins
## Ingredients:
Pancake mix

eggs

water

## Equipment needed:
Whisk,

Bowl

Pan

Stove

## Steps for preparation:
1. Grab your ingredients

2. Mix the ingredients in the bowl

3. Once the batter is mixed, we will put the batter onto the pan (on the stove) for 2 mins on each side
