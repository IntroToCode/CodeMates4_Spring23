# How to make Pancakes

## Servings: 7

## Time to prepare: 
15 mins
## Ingredients:
Pancake mix
eggs
water

## Equipment needed:
Whisk 
Bowl
Pan
Stove

## Steps for preparation:
Grab your ingredients
Mix the ingredients in the bowl
Once the batter is mixed, we will put the batter onto the pan (on the stove) for 2 mins on each side
